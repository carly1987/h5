It is a great place to set-up a desk space and work with your team, or to make important connections. Get involved with DSkies creative collective if you are looking to enhance you career in design.

We provide independent creatives a place to get work done, meet clients and professionally “show up”, both mentally and physically. Work independently while being part of a community of talented individuals sharing values and synergy.

Flexible, dynamic and comfortable As coworking spaces, DSkies offer amenities and services that let the creative live and feel a comfortable space like his own home. Food, coffee and beverages, fast wi-fi, administration services, printing, amusement , as well as all restaurants and all services offered by several businesses located in the basement of SOHO Fuxing plaza.

A workspace where exhibit your talentWe think that one of the first service a designer needs is the chance to share, highlight and exhibit his own talent and work with the others. That’s why our workspaces are conceived in order to show designers' works, products and researches.

The best location for best designersWith the first office located at the 11th floor of the tower A of SOHO Fuxing plaza in the middle of Xintiandi, DSkies want to let talended designers work in areas opened to international sharing of ideas and initiatives about the world of design, fashion and technology.
